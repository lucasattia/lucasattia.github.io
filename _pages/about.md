I am currently an ML researcher working with Eli Lilly and Company on the [Lilly TuneLab](https://tunelab.lilly.com/) platform. Prior to joining Lilly, I earned my PhD in chemical engineering from [MIT](https://cheme.mit.edu/) working in the [Doyle Group](https://doylegroup.mit.edu/). Funded as a [DOE Computational  Science Graduate Fellow](https://www.krellinst.org/csgf/about-doe-csgf), my dissertation focused on drug nanoparticle processing and spanned nanotechnology, molecular simulation, and cheminformatics. I earned my B.S.E in chemical engineering from the University of Delaware. I am passionate about leveraging computational modeling to guide, inform, and accelerate the design of novel molecules and materials. An accomplished scientific communicator, I have contributed to 12+ peer-reviewed publications and 60+ presentations at conferences and symposia, and have delivered 12+ award-winning presentations. I enjoy fostering collaboration and mentorship within research environments.
